ü•ú Cashew Processing Stages

  • RCN Procurement: Raw Cashew Nut purchasing with moisture testing and quality verification
  • Storage & Preparation: Ventilated warehouse storage with humidity control to prevent mold
  • Cleaning & Roasting: Remove foreign matter, roast to facilitate shell removal
  • Shelling & Cutting: Extract kernels (highest shrinkage risk, labor-intensive)
  • Drying: Reduce moisture to ~3% at 80¬∞C for preservation and quality
  • Peeling: Remove testa (seed coat) with initial quality sorting
  • Grading & Sorting: Final classification by size and quality (W180, W210, W240, W320)
  • Packing & Export: Vacuum packaging, labeling, export documentation

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Risk Points & HERA Controls

  • Moisture Manipulation: HERA tracks moisture tests with actor stamping and audit trails
  • Shrinkage & Pilferage: Real-time yield tracking with input/output reconciliation
  • Union Resistance: Gradual automation tracking with productivity metrics
  • Quality Control: AQL-based inspection workflows with compliance documentation
  • Cost Management: Real-time cost center tracking with profitability analysis

üìä Key Performance Indicators

  • Yield Rate: Kernel output as percentage of RCN input (industry benchmarks by grade)
  • Shrinkage Tracking: Real-time monitoring of material losses during processing
  • Grade Distribution: Percentage breakdown by export grades (impacts revenue directly)
  • Quality Metrics: Rejection rates, moisture content, foreign matter levels
  • Cost Analysis: Cost per kg by grade, labor productivity, overhead allocation
  • Compliance Status: Export certifications, quality standards, audit readiness

üîó HERA System Integration Points

  • Sacred Six Schema: All data stored in 6 universal tables, zero custom tables needed
  • Dynamic Data Fields: Lot-specific information (moisture %, grade mix) in core_dynamic_data
  • Universal Transactions: Material movements, labor hours, overhead allocations tracked
  • Actor Stamping: Complete audit trail - who did what, when, and why
  • Organization Isolation: Multi-tenant security with perfect data separation
  • Real-time API: Live integration with scales, moisture meters, quality instruments
